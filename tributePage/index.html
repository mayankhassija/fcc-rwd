Michael Jackson

The King of Pop (1958–2009)

Michael Jackson performing in 1988

Achievements

  • 13 Grammy Awards
  • **Best-selling album of all time** (Thriller, 70 million copies claimed globally)
  • **39 Guinness World Records**
  • **26 American Music Awards** – most ever
  • Inducted twice into the **Rock and Roll Hall of Fame** (as a solo artist and with the Jackson 5)

Iconic Albums

  • Off the Wall (1979)
  • Thriller (1982)
  • Bad (1987)
  • Dangerous (1991)
  • HIStory (1995)
  • Invincible (2001)

Messages He Spread

“Heal the world, make it a better place…”

— Michael Jackson, “Heal the World”

Through music and charity, Michael promoted:

  • Global unity & love
  • Children’s rights (e.g., through his Heal the World Foundation)
  • Environmental awareness (e.g., "Earth Song")
  • Racial equality
